Grading

Grade A  are the ideal conditions for services: They will not misuse your data and will treat you properly while upholding your rights.

 

 

Grade B  Although the terms of service are fair to the user, they may be made better.

 

 

Grade C  The terms of service are acceptable, however there are a few things to take into account.

 

 

Grade D  There are several significant issues that require your attention, or the terms of service are quite inconsistent.

 

 

Grade E  The terms of service give rise to grave issues.
