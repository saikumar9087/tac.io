About TACS


Tacs is a website to help and acknowledge the users about the policies given by the software. The policies maybe about the data privacy, but most of the time we ignore them and simply agree the terms. for that we have selected main terms of usage of an app/software and displayed it on a page for ease of reading.

Terms and conditions

Tacs is a project started in june 2022 to help and acknowledge the users, "what will happen if we ignore and agree to terms and policy of an software".
